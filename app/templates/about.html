About EmotionLens

Our Mission

EmotionLens is dedicated to helping people understand and track their emotional states using cutting-edge facial recognition technology. Our mission is to provide intuitive, accurate, and privacy-focused emotion recognition tools that can improve emotional intelligence and well-being.

The Technology

Our emotion recognition system is powered by a state-of-the-art deep learning model trained on extensive datasets of facial expressions. We use MobileNetV2 architecture fine-tuned for seven key emotions:

Angry

Detecting frustration and displeasure

Disgust

Recognizing aversion reactions

Fear

Identifying anxious expressions

Happy

Measuring joyful expressions

Neutral

Baseline expressions

Sad

Detecting melancholy and distress

Surprise

Recognizing astonishment

Privacy Focus

We understand the sensitivity of facial data. All processing happens on our secure servers, and you have full control over your data. Our privacy mode allows you to process emotions without storing any facial images.

We do not share your data with third parties and employ industry-standard encryption to protect all information.

The Team

EmotionLens was developed by a team of passionate AI engineers, UX designers, and psychology researchers committed to creating technology that enhances human understanding.
